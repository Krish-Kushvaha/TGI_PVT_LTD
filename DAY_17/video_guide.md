# AI Video Generation Guide

## Tools
- Runway ML: https://runwayml.com
- Pika Labs: https://pika.art
- Synthesia: https://synthesia.io

## Steps
1. Sign up on Runway ML
2. Choose Text to Video
3. Write a detailed prompt
4. Generate and download

## Sample Prompts
- "Futuristic robot in neon city, cinematic"
- "Professional presenting in modern office"
